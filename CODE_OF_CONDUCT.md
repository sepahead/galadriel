# Code of conduct

Galadriel is a research project. Discussion must remain precise, respectful, and
safe for participants with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

## Expected conduct

- Critique claims, methods, evidence, and code without attacking people.
- State uncertainty, conflicts of interest, and safety or security limitations.
- Respect privacy and do not publish credentials, private data, or uncoordinated
  vulnerability details.
- Welcome good-faith correction and preserve negative results.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, and sustained
  disruption.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to Sepehr Mahmoudian at `sepmhn@gmail.com`.
Security vulnerabilities must instead use the private process in `SECURITY.md`.
The maintainer may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or ban a
participant when necessary to protect the project and its community. Reports will
be handled as confidentially as a fair review permits. Retaliation is prohibited.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when someone represents the project
in public. Enforcement decisions should be documented privately with the evidence
needed for consistent follow-up.
