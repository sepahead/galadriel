# Code of conduct

Galadriel is a research project. Keep all discussions precise and respectful.
Make the project safe for participants with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected conduct

- Critique claims, methods, evidence, and code. Do not attack people.
- State uncertainties, conflicts of interest, and safety or security limitations.
- Respect privacy. Do not publish credentials, private data, or uncoordinated vulnerability details.
- Welcome good-faith corrections and preserve negative results.
- Do not use harassment, discrimination, threats, or sexualized conduct.
- Do not cause sustained disruption.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to Sepehr Mahmoudian at `sepmhn@gmail.com`.
For security vulnerabilities, use the private process in `SECURITY.md`.
The maintainer can edit or remove contributions to protect the project and its community.
The maintainer can also restrict or prohibit participation.
The maintainer will keep reports confidential to the extent that a fair review permits.
Do not retaliate.

This policy applies in repository spaces.
It also applies when a person represents the project in public.
Document enforcement decisions privately with the evidence that consistent follow-up requires.
